Mercedes-Benz CLS63 AMG

Unmatched Power and Elegance

CLS63 AMG combines aggressive performance with sophisticated luxury.

The Mercedes-Benz CLS63 AMG stands as a pinnacle of high-performance luxury, marrying the bold aesthetics of a four-door coupe with the raw power of AMG engineering. As part of Mercedes’ AMG lineup, the CLS63 exemplifies the brand’s commitment to "Unmatched Power and Elegance," offering drivers a rare combination of aggressive performance and sophisticated refinemen.Sleek Coupe Silhouette: The CLS63’s low-slung roofline, muscular fenders, and frameless doors redefine the sedan segment with a coupe-like profile. AMG-specific touches—such as a Panamericana grille (on newer models), quad exhaust tips, and aerodynamic body kits—signal its performance pedigree. Luxury Interior: Handcrafted materials like Nappa leather, brushed aluminum trim, and ambient lighting create a cockpit tailored for both comfort and adrenaline. The driver-centric layout includes AMG performance seats and a flat-bottom steering wheel. Acceleration: 0–60 mph in 3.6–3.9 seconds, with an electronically limited top speed of 155–186 mph (with optional AMG Driver’s Package). Engine: Depending on the model year, the CLS63 features either a 5.5L V8 biturbo (W218 generation, 2012–2017) or a 4.0L V8 biturbo (W218 facelift and newer), delivering 536–585 HP (up to 621 HP in the S-Model). Technology: Equipped with AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT transmission, AIRMATIC adaptive suspension, and rear-wheel-drive bias (with 4MATIC all-wheel drive available for).
